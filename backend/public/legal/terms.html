The marketplace

Bridger operates a peer-to-peer marketplace that connects senders who need to ship a package with travelers who have spare luggage capacity along a published route. Bridger is not a party to the underlying carriage contract between sender and traveler.

Sender obligations

  • Accurately describe the package, its contents, weight, and declared value.
  • Comply with all applicable customs, export, and import regulations.
  • Pay the agreed price in full when matched with a traveler.

Traveler obligations

  • Inspect the package at pickup; refuse anything that violates Section 5.
  • Hand the package over only to the verified receiver code.
  • Maintain reasonable care during transit.

Payments & escrow

When a deal is matched, the sender's payment is held in an escrow account by Bridger's payment processor. Funds are released to the traveler only once the receiver confirms delivery via the unique receiver code or once the dispute window expires.

  • Service fees are disclosed before checkout and cannot change after a deal is created.
  • Refunds for cancellations follow the cancellation matrix shown in-app.
  • Withdrawals to your linked bank account or Stripe Connect arrive within 1–3 business days.

Disputes & resolution
