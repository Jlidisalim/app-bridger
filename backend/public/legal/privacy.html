Privacy Policy

At a glance

  • We collect only what we need to run the marketplace, settle payments, and prevent fraud.
  • We never sell your personal data to advertisers or data brokers.
  • You can export or delete your data at any time from Profile → Danger Zone → Delete Account.
  • Biometric data (face embeddings) stays encrypted at rest and is processed only to verify identity.

How we use your data

  • Provide the Service. Match senders with travelers, route messages, calculate prices, and track shipments.
  • Verify identity. KYC documents and face embeddings prevent impersonation and money-laundering.
  • Process payments. Hold funds in escrow, settle disputes, pay travelers via Stripe Connect.
  • Prevent fraud. Trust scores, ML review of listings, and IP/device fingerprints flag suspicious activity.
  • Communicate. Transactional notifications about your deals (push, SMS, email).
  • Improve the product. Aggregated, de-identified analytics on funnel completion and feature adoption.
We do not use your data to train third-party advertising or generative AI models.

Legal basis (GDPR Art. 6)

  • Contract — to deliver the marketplace you signed up for.
  • Legal obligation — KYC and AML record-keeping under EU and Tunisian financial law.
  • Legitimate interest — fraud prevention, security monitoring, and product analytics.
  • Consent — biometric verification, marketing communications (opt-in only).

Retention

  • Active account data — for as long as your account remains active.
  • Deletion requests — personal identifiers anonymised within 7 days; counterparty-facing records (deal IDs, ratings) retained in tombstoned form for 7 years to satisfy financial-audit obligations.
  • Biometric embeddings — deleted immediately upon account deletion.
  • Server access logs — 30 days, then automatically purged.
  • Backups — encrypted, rolling 90-day window.

Security

We use industry-standard safeguards to protect your data:

  • TLS 1.3 in transit, AES-256 encryption at rest;
  • Stripe handles all card numbers — they never touch our servers;
  • Face embeddings stored as opaque vectors, never as raw images, in pgvector with restricted role access;
  • Two-factor authentication for staff, audit logs for every admin action;
  • Quarterly third-party penetration tests and continuous Sentry monitoring.

Your rights

Wherever you are located, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you;
  • Correct inaccurate information from Profile → Personal Information;
  • Erase your account and associated personal data from Profile → Danger Zone → Delete Account;
  • Port your data — we provide a JSON export on request;
  • Object to specific processing activities, including profiling for fraud scores;
  • Withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.
To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@bridger.app. We respond within 30 days, as required by GDPR Art. 12(3).

International transfers

Where data leaves the European Economic Area — for example to Stripe in the United States — we rely on the Standard Contractual Clauses adopted by the European Commission and on the EU-US Data Privacy Framework, supplemented by the technical measures listed in Security.

Contact & complaints

For privacy questions or to exercise your rights, contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@bridger.app.

If you believe we have not handled your personal data lawfully, you may lodge a complaint with the Tunisian National Authority for the Protection of Personal Data (INPDP) or with your local EU data protection supervisor.
